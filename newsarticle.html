Winter Glow at Wolske Bay Park - February 8th

Join us for a FREE community celebration at Wolske Bay Park! Experience the magic of winter with:

Seasonal Light Displays – Stroll through glowing lights and capture beautiful photo memories.

All-Day Ice Skating – Fun for all ages! A variety of skate sizes will be available. (Loaner skates provided by American Edge Real Estate).

The free skates are a total life saver!

Getting There: Park at Lakeside Park and the FREE shuttle transportation will run to and from Lakeside Park from 4 pm - 9 pm as Wolske Bay Road will be closed for the event. Limited handicap parking available at Wolske Bay Park.

Don't miss the chance to experience a day full of winter fun! For more information, visit the city of Menomonie's Facebook page.
